2025 NASA Micro-g NExT: ASTROID

Overview

ASTROID (Adhesive Stamping Technology for Regolith Orientation Integrity Delineation) is a lunar sampling tool designed for NASA’s Artemis mission to preserve regolith grain orientation at the moon’s south pole. Its ergonomic, stamp-like mechanism uses a lab-developed adhesive gel to collect surface particles under extreme conditions, enabling precise scientific analysis and advancing understanding of lunar surface processes.
